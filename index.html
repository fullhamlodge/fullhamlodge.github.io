Camps Bay Guest House

Fullham Lodge offers home away from home Camps Bay accommodation, the guest house offers a choice of self-catering apartments or bed and breakfast accommodation in Camps Bay The guest house is located on a quiet edge of the Table Mountain Nature Reserve overlooking the Atlantic Ocean.

Accommodation options available in the guest house include bed and breakfast rooms,(Dolphin and Whale Suites) with sea facing balconies and en-suite bathrooms, a self-catering apartment, (Pool Suite) leading straight out onto the pool and Jacuzzi deck or a private self-catering cottage, (Garden Suite) with a patio area. Stunning views over the Atlantic Ocean are to be had throughout the guest house.

Camps Bay Self Catering or Bed and Breakfast Accommodation

Conveniently located in Camps Bay, one of the most popular and sought after areas of Cape Town, the guest house offers a range of excellent Camps Bay accommodation options, the self-catering or bed and breakfast suites will suit all tastes and budgets.

The shared lounge, eating areas and the pool are there for the pleasure of all our guests.

A delicious leisurely, buffet style breakfast is included in the tariff. Free Wi Fi is available throughout the house. Visa and Mastercard accepted. Children over the age of 12 are welcome.
 
"A home that shelters the body and comforts the soul in the heart of Cape Town's Platinum mile."
