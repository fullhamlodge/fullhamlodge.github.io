Dolphin Suite

  • Bed and breakfast suite
  • Spacious room with king size bed can also be a twin
  • En suite bathroom with corner bath/shower over bath with mountain view
  • Large balcony with sea view
  • Mini bar fridge
  • Tea/coffee facilities
  • Television with M-net and satellite
  • Aircon

The Dolphin Suite, much like the Pool and Whale suites, has been designed with the needs of the guest firmly in mind. The spacious bedroom is adjoined by a large en suite bathroom with a choice of a bath or shower. A large balcony overlooking the sea allows you to soak up the rich ambience which makes Cape Town such a much loved destination.
